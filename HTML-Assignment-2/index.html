What Does a QA Engineer Do?

This website is all about QA & Automation Engineering. Here you will get information regarding this. The main role of QA is quality assurance. A QA engineer focuses on improving software development processes and preventing defects in production. In other words, they make sure the software development team is doing the right things the right way. The QA engineer job scope embraces a number of duties.
